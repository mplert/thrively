At Thrively Health, we believe in the business case for gender equality and health equity. Gender gaps, embedded in the behavior of the family, the market, and society, affects prospects for poverty reduction and growth by stimulating productivity and earnings and improving child development outcomes.

Our team of designers, technologists, economists, educators, public health professionals, and physicians take a unique approach to working alongside community members, local organizations, businesses, and government in diagnosing, creating, testing, and scaling product and service solutions for the global problems facing our most vulnerable communities.

Our first product, Mo Mo, is a mobile platform that crowdsources health and health-enabling resources to support new families through the new life experience.

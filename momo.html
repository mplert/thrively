MO MO

MAE SOT, THAILAND

User using Mo Mo in Mae Sot, Thailand

Mo Mo is a mobile platform that crowdsources health and health-enabling resources to support new families through the new life experience.

Mo Mo was launched during summer of 2018 in Mae Sot, Thailand in collaboration with local clinic. Mae Sot is the district in Tak Province of Thailand, across the border from Myawaddy in Kayin State, Myanmar. Mae Sot has experienced a lot of influx of Myanmar migrants, historically due to internal conflicts within the border state which results in displacement of its population. The pilot targets new families among Myanmar migrants living in Mae Sot district as they are the most vulnerable population within the district.

The results of the pilot are currently being processed and analyzed.
